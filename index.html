About

Here on this page, we will cover what a computer is and what a laptop is. Not only are we going to explain what they are, but we are also going to compare the difference and similarities between the two. Then at the end of the page we are going to say what we think is better between the two. Remember it is just our opinion and not the truth!

What is a Computer?

A computer is a little more than just an electronic device that processes data and performs calculations. To some it is a device that stores all their personal data from home movies, pictures of friends and family, important documents, and more! To others it is a tool to complete tasks and solve problems, which may be for personal or for professional reasons.
A computer typically consist of a case (tower that can range in size from small to large), a monitor, a keyboard, and a mouse. Some computers internally have a hard drive, a power supply, motherboard, RAM (speed of the computer),CPU (the brains of the computer), and other components depending on the user's needs. Needs to be constantly plugged into the outlet to power on/off, requires a stationary area to be set up, cables are easily exposed and can be tangled easily, has a significant about of extra ports not just for internal upgrades but also for external devices, and can easily be upgraded or downgraded.

What is a laptop?

A laptop is a portable computer typically has a built in display, keyboard, and touchpad (mouse.) Just as the computer, a laptop can be used for personal and professional reasons.
Laptops are designed to be easy to carry around and to be used in various locations. They have a battery attached to them, which allows them to be used without having to be plugged into an outlet constantly. Because the laptop has it's own built in display, keyboard, and touchpad, it does not require users to use external hook ups such as a separate monitor, keyboard, or mouse. Laptops can also be upgraded as well and have some external ports for connecting devices.

Comparison

Computers are typically larger and more powerful than laptops, while laptops are more portable and convenient for travel.

Summary

In summary, both computers and laptops have their own advantages and disadvantages, and the choice between them depends on the user's needs and preferences.
